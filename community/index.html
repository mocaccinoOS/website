Join the MocaccinoOS community

MocaccinoOS is an open source project that anyone in the community can use, improve, and enjoy. We'd love you to join us! Here's a few ways to find out what's happening and get involved.

Learn and Connect

Using or want to use MocaccinoOS? Find out more here:

  • Forum: Join our community in the Forum!
  • Discord: Join us on Discord!
  • Signal: Join us on the official Signal group!
  • Telegram: Join us on the official Telegram group!
  • Twitter: Follow us on Twitter!
  • Matrix chat: Chat with other project users

Develop and Contribute

If you want to get more involved by contributing to MocaccinoOS, join us here:

  • GitHub: Development takes place here!
  • Slack: Join the development channel on slack

You can find out how to contribute to these docs in our Contribution Guidelines.
